About Albean

Music that
moves people.

We created Albean after going to countless weddings and socials with DJs that completely missed the mark and didn't read the room.

We are Albert and Connor, and are passionate about creating unforgettable moments through music.

Whether it's the first dance at your wedding, a packed dance floor at a social, or a lowkey cocktail hour at your corporate event, we tailor every set to the room and the moment.

We meet with you, listen, and understand your music taste, your crowd, and the vibe you're going for. And yes, we also take requests.
